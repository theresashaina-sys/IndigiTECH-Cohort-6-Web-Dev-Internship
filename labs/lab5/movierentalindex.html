How it Works:

FlickLend is a peer to peer media rental community that allows users to rent and lend movies, music, and games to each other. Users can create profiles, list their available media, and browse what others have to offer in their local area. Communication is facilitated through an integrated chat system, making it easy to arrange rentals and returns. Register today to start sharing your favorite media with others and discover new titles from your community!
